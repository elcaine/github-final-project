Code of Conduct
Our Pledge
Contributors and maintainers pledge to create an open and welcoming community and a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of various personal characteristics. The goal is a positive and constructive environment for all members. 
Our Standards
Positive behaviors include using welcoming language, being respectful of different viewpoints, accepting constructive criticism, attempting collaboration before conflict, focusing on the community's best interests, and showing empathy. 
Unacceptable behaviors include sexualized language or advances, trolling, insulting comments, harassment, publishing private information ("doxxing"), and other unprofessional conduct. 
Our Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for defining acceptable behavior standards and taking fair action against unacceptable behavior. They can remove or reject contributions that don't follow the Code of Conduct and can ban contributors temporarily or permanently for inappropriate behavior. 
Enforcement
Violations can be reported to the project team at [xxx]. Complaints will be investigated confidentially. Consequences can range from a warning to temporary suspension or permanent expulsion.
